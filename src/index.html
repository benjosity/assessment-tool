The AppDynamics Business Observability Platform

Welcome to the AppDynamics Business Observability Journey Mapper.

Technologists today need to be able to contextualize IT performance insights with real-time business data to prioritize actions based on customer and business impact. This is what we call Business Observability.

But few technologists currently have the strategy or tools to truly connect IT performance with business outcomes and 73% are concerned that this will be detrimental to their organization in the next year.

Technologists have no option but to urgently begin the journey to link full-stack observability with business context within their organizations. The Business Observability Journey Mapper identifies 6 key stages in this journey.

By answering seven short questions, we’ll assess your organization’s current capabilities and we’ll give you advice on how you can speed up your journey so that you can cut through complexity and observe what matters most.
